Climate

  1. Castle-Rock is known for excessive rains and thick forest cover. It provides totally pollution free environment. Winter is quite chilly and temperature drops to 6 to 7 degrees.

    Hornbill Homestay is located in the middle of thick forest. A variety of birds and animals such as Hornbill, King-Fisher, Whistling Thrush, Giant Red Squirrels, Langur etc. can be seen in and around the camp area.

    The animals such as Barking Dear, Sambar, Bison, Wild Dogs, Wild Boar, Fox, Leopard, Bear and if you are lucky then even a Tiger.
